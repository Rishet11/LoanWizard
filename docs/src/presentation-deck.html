Apply for a loan by talking to your camera. The model watches, listens, verifies, and prices the offer in minutes, not days.
AI video loan origination for Indian digital lending
Rishet Mehra  ·  github.com/Rishet11/LoanWizard
The problem
Loan origination in India still runs on paper and patience.
SLOW

Days, not minutes

Branch visits, photocopied documents, hand-keyed forms, multi-day waits before a decision.

OPAQUE

A black box

The applicant is told yes or no with no reason they can see, and no record the lender can defend.

BOLTED ON

Video as theatre

Video-KYC made remote onboarding legal, but most products bolt a call onto the same old paper process.

The decision stays a black box, and the audit trail is thin. That is the gap.
LoanWizard02
The solution
The interview is the application.
SPEAK

The form fills itself

Multilingual speech-to-text extracts name, employment, income, amount and purpose as you talk.

VERIFY

The camera checks you live

Passive liveness — blink, head-pose, texture — plus on-device Aadhaar and PAN OCR. No uploads.

PRICE

Real-time decision

A risk MLP, a fraud MLP, a persona model and a policy engine produce a rate, an EMI and reason codes.

PROVE

Explainable & replayable

Every offer ships reason codes and a narrative; the input snapshot is frozen so it can be re-run.

One continuous experience — and compliance is first class, not an afterthought.

LoanWizard06
The moat · explainable AI
Explanations derived from the model — not dressed up to look like it.

Permutation importance

  • Baseline prediction, then perturb each of 12 features to its scaled mean, re-predict, measure the change.
  • Runs as a single batched predict — cheap enough for every request.
  • Reason codes reflect what the model actually responds to, not raw feature size.

Replay on the exact version

  • Each decision is stamped with its model versions.
  • Weights are archived per version; replay loads the model as it was.
  • Reports exact_model_match — and flags it honestly on fallback.
Models are trained on synthetic data with scripts in the repo, so the committed weights are reproducible from scratch.

Fair lending

LoanWizard08
Demo honesty
What is real, and what is mocked.

Real & running

  • Full ML pipeline: risk MLP, fraud MLP, persona, policy, narrator
  • Permutation-importance reason codes
  • Audit + frozen snapshots + replay on exact version
  • Browser perception locally: liveness, OCR, age, speech
  • Admin: sessions, fairness, drift, replay

Mocked / optional by design

  • Bureau pull — adapter in place, no commercial credentials
  • LLM narration — template default, Gemini optional
  • Whisper STT model — opt-in deploy toggle (fallback wired)
  • Public judge link — scripted perception + reliable mock offer
We would rather be precise than oversell. The real camera and ML path runs locally and the ML Space serves the live /offer, /docs and /fairness endpoints.
